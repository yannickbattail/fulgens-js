Welcome to AIlurus Fulgens, a coding game where you build your AI.
Why the name AIlurus Fulgens?
It's the latin name of the specie of the cute red panda.
It also begin with AI for Artificial Intelligence.
Ailurus is the part of the project that manage the maps and the drones of every players
Fulgens is the graphic interface where you AI runs.
The Blocks in this page
Map: display of the map Factories, mines and your drones.
Drones: status and position of your drones, progression of the current instruction.
Map info: description of the map and quantity of resources to finish the level.
Player: player stats and current quantity of resources int the factory.
Manual action: a small form to manualy send instructions to you drones.
Output: output of the execution of you code.
AI code: the code of you IA.
AilurusApi (how to get map and drone info and send instructions)
Send instructions:
ailurusApi.instructions(instructions , fctOnSuccess, fctOnError);
instructions: [{'TYPE': 'MoveTo', 'DroneName': 'Drone_1','Destination': {"X":45, "Y":45}}];
Get map info:
ailurusApi.ailurusApi.map(function (map) { doSomthingWithTheMap(map);}, fctOnError);

Get player and drones info:
ailurusApi.ailurusApi.playerContext(function (player) { doSomthingWithThePlayer(player);}, fctOnError);

For more info see: https://github.com/yannickbattail/fulgens-js/blob/master/docs/ailurus-api.md
